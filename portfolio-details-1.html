CNSC Auxiliary/IGP Web-Based System is designed for the IGP / Auxiliary Services of Camarines Norte State College. It focuses on the improvement of the Auxiliary Servicesâ€™ transactions which will be converted to a web-based system. It can be accessed by public users, students, employees, other government agencies, IGP staff and other stakeholders. This web-based system is designed to monitor the reports and check the availability of all the services of the CNSC Auxiliary/IGP. It will also notify the administrator if a student or a public user wants to rent a room at the College Dormitory, Amphitheater, Pavilion, and/or the two mini buses. This system will also notify clients on the availability of their requested service and billing statement will be sent for dorm occupancy.
