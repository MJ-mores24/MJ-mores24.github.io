Floorcenter Employee Portal

Details

  • Category: Web development
  • Client: JBW Floorcenter Inc.
  • Project date: August - November 2022
  • Project URL: portal.floorcenter.com

For my first project at JBW, Floorcenter Inc, I had the privilege of developing the Employee Portal. This comprehensive platform was crafted using HTML, CSS, Javascript, and PHP, with a backend supported by an MSSQL database and hosted on IIS.

The project's scope encompasses various departments, including HR, Accounting, Admin, Maintenance, Marketing, Logistics, and Sales Support. Notably, the portal features a convenient payslip that can view in a specific date.
