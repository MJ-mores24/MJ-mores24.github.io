AMD Logo

Details

  • Category: Design
  • Client: AMD Transport Service
  • Project date: October 2023

The logo was crafted using Adobe Photoshop, seamlessly merging the vector image of L300 with the initials of the owner. The client actively participated in selecting the color palette, and through careful color blending and illustration, I arrived at the final design. The end result reflects a harmonious combination of the client's vision and my creative input.
